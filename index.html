About the Conference

The International Conference on Generative AI and Data Analytics: Exploring a Smarter Future (GAIDA 2025) aims to bring together global experts, researchers, academicians, and professionals to exchange insights on the latest innovations in Generative AI and Data Analytics. Scheduled for 20th and 21st August 2025 at the University College of Engineering, BIT Campus, Tiruchirappalli, the conference provides a platform for showcasing cutting-edge research, fostering interdisciplinary collaboration, and exploring impactful solutions across industries. With keynote speakers from premier institutions worldwide and sessions covering diverse themes like AI in healthcare, cybersecurity, IoT, and sustainability, GAIDA 2025 seeks to drive academic excellence and real-world innovation in emerging technologies.
